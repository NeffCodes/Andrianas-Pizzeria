About Us

Andriana's Pizzeria brings you the best pizza and wings in South Carolina. Founder Andriana Ricci is the proud descendant of her great grandfather, Gino Ricci, who immigrated from Cortona, Italy. Gino's specialty pizzas were so popular with his family that he began his own restaurant. Today Andriana and her brother continue his old world inspired recipes for you to experience.
